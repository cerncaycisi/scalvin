# Support

## Usage questions

Use [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/cerncaycisi/scalvin/discussions)
for public usage and design questions. Use synthetic examples only.

## Bugs

Use the repository's structured bug form. Include the Scalvin commit/version,
OS, Node version, client, exact command, and a minimal reproduction without
personal data.

## Security or safety bypasses

Use [private vulnerability reporting](https://github.com/cerncaycisi/scalvin/security/advisories/new).
Do not open a public issue first.

## Personal or crisis support

The project issue tracker and maintainers are not a therapy or crisis service.
Do not post crisis disclosures or private histories in GitHub. If someone may
be in immediate danger, use local emergency or crisis resources appropriate to
their location.
